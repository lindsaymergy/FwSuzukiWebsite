Welcome to Fort Wayne Suzuki Talent Education!

Located in Fort Wayne, IN, we provide classical Suzuki instruction to area students.

Modeled on the process a child uses to learn how to speak, the Suzuki method has been developed with ease of learning in mind.

About Us

Proudly Continuing Tradition

Our program offers instruction in violin, viola and cello to students age 3 and older.

Besides individual lessons, students participate in weekly group and theory classes.

After mastering the fundamental skills, students participate in Beginning Orchestra.

Students move on to the Intermediate and then Advanced Orchestra as their skills progress.

Advanced students also participate in Chamber Music Ensembles and String Choirs. Details here.
